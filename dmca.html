🚨 DMCA Takedown Policy

Last Updated: 9.2.2025

1. Introduction

We respect the intellectual property rights of others. If you believe that your copyrighted work has been used without permission, you may request a takedown under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

2. How to Request a Takedown

You can either send an email OR use the form below to report copyrighted content.

📧 Option 1: Email

Send an email to 📧 unlimitedtabsofficial@gmail.com with:

  • 🔹 Your Contact Information (Full Name, Email, Company)
  • 🔹 Description of Copyrighted Work (Song Name, Official Source)
  • 🔹 URL of the Infringing Content
  • 🔹 Statements of Good Faith & Accuracy
  • 🔹 Your Digital Signature

📝 Option 2: Submit a Takedown Request Online









3. What Happens After a DMCA Request?

Once we receive a valid DMCA request, we will:

  • ✅ Review the request within 48 hours.
  • ✅ Remove the infringing content if the claim is valid.
  • ✅ If the request is invalid or lacks required details, we will ask for more information.
